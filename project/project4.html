Stress in Human

Stress is a condition of physical and psychological tension caused by the perception of fear and anxiety. Stress is the reaction of the organism to unpleasant stimuli (stimulation), stress should be understood as an interactive relation that occurs between physical, physiological, psychological and behavioral systems

What is Heart Rate ?

In medicine, the number of times the heart beats within a certain time period, usually a minute. The heart rate can be felt at the wrist, side of the neck, back of the knees, top of the foot, groin, and other places in the body where an artery is close to the skin. The resting heart rate is normally between 60 and 100 beats a minute in a healthy adult who is at rest.

What is RR Interval ?

RR Interval is simply a measure of the variation in time between each heartbeat. This variation is controlled by a primitive part of the nervous system called the autonomic nervous system (ANS). It works behind the scenes, automatically regulating our heart rate, blood pressure, breathing, and digestion among other key tasks.
